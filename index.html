Privacy Policy

BAP: Discover Korean Cuisine
Last updated: March 16, 2026

BAP does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All features work entirely offline on your device.

Overview

BAP: Discover Korean Cuisine ("BAP", "the App") is a mobile application that helps foreign visitors in South Korea understand Korean food menus. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

BAP is designed to operate fully offline without any server or cloud infrastructure. We do not collect, process, or share:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Camera images or photos
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

On-Device Features

The following features run entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server:

  • Menu Scan (Camera/OCR) — The camera is used solely to capture and recognize Korean text on menus using on-device OCR (Google ML Kit). Images are processed locally and are never uploaded or stored.
  • Text-to-Speech (TTS) — Korean ordering phrases are spoken using your device's built-in TTS engine. No audio data is transmitted.
  • Saved Foods & Preferences — Your saved items, allergy presets, and app settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Local Notifications — Meal review reminders are scheduled locally. No push notification servers are involved.

Third-Party Services

The App may include the following third-party services in future updates:

  • Google AdMob — For displaying advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users who purchase the ad-free upgrade will not be served any ads.
  • RevenueCat — For processing in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data as described in RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

These services operate independently from BAP's core functionality. You can use the App's core features (food guide, menu scan, TTS ordering) without any data collection.

Children's Privacy

BAP is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Security

Since BAP does not collect or transmit user data, there is no risk of data breach from our side. All user preferences are stored locally on your device and are subject to your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
